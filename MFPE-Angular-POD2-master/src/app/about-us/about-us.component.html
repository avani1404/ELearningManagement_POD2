E-Learning is where lifelong learners come to learn the skills they need, to land the jobs they want, to build the lives they deserve.

E-Learning management system is a software application for the administration, documentation, tracking, reporting, automation and delivery of educational courses, training programs, or learning and development programs. E-learning can also be termed as a network enabled transfer of skills and knowledge, and the delivery of education is made to a large number of recipients at the same or different times.
